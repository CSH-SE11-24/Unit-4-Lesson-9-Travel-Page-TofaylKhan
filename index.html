New York

New York is a diverse and dynamic state in the northeastern United States. Known as the "Empire State," it is home to the iconic New York City, a global financial and cultural hub. The state features landmarks like Niagara Falls, the Statue of Liberty, and the Catskill Mountains. With a rich history, including the Erie Canal and Ellis Island, New York is recognized for its cultural institutions, Broadway theaters, and as a major center for commerce and immigration. .
